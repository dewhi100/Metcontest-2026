Fusion charged shot flare, by Tundain.

Adds the flare projectile when firing a charged shot from fusion to super metroid.
When firing a charged shot it will appear at the tip of the arm cannon, dealing twice the damage of a charged shot from your current beam. This can be used to deal more dmg when firing a charged shot from point-blank range at enemies.

Important! In order to have enough space to fit in the gfx for the flare, you need to apply the misc. sprites gfx free up patch as well, this patch is normally also included. Make sure to apply both patches properly.
