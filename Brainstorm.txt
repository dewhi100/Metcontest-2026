The setup: Samus is crashed into an ocean planet.

Start in a flooded ship, and descend through more and more decrepit past wrecks all pancaked on top of each other.
Includes a pirate ship which crashed at the same time as you.
Deep down under all the wreckage is bona fide ruins and the "Atlantis Magnet" that's keeping you here.



Electrolysis:

Gradually gain oxygen reserves while underwater. Or instantly while in air.
The more tanks you collect, the faster you charge.

What costs Oxygen?

Breathing- 
	with one tank, it cancels out underwater generation. (but you can still breathe)
	in deep water, it costs double (enough to drain a single tank, or cancel two tanks)

Suitless Space Jump
Gravity Boost
Buoy ball
Hammerball
Dash
Pseudogravity effect


breathing: run out of air, and you take damage.
Samus' suit has a built in air reserve, but doesn't replenish O2.
You have a timer before you start to take damage.
One electolysis tank increases suit capacity and is enough to offset breathing.
Even if you are at zero storage, you don't drown as O2 is keeping up with you.
You just cant vent any for tricks.
At two tanks and up, the O2 generation is a net positive.
In deep water, breathing cost doubles. One tank is not enough to stop you from suffocating.
Two will keep up with you.
